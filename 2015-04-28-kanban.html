Current Questions

  • Is [tool x] up to date?
  • Do you have something to do?
  • Do you have problems?

Yes, Yes, No

Do not turn it into zombie-mode

Better Questions

Keep them focused on deliverables

  • What have you done yesterday?
  • What will you do today?

Additional Questions

Context dependent questions

  • After x days: Are you blocked? Do you need help?
  • Who will you work together with?

Current Visualisation

  • In function of supporting SM7
  • Mirrors SM7
  • Lots of people dislike SM7

Better Visualisation

Support developers in their flow

  • Todo - A big bucket of possible work
  • Doing - Limited to the amount of people/pairs
  • Blocked - Blocked by anyone not part of the team
  • Test - Optionally in case testing is done outside the team
  • Done - The work is really done for the developer

Collaboration

Kanban is about collaboration

  • Working together on an issue helps sharing domain knowledge
  • Working together on an issue increases technical skill
  • Working together on an issue improves communication
